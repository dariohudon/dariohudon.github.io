# Notes from the edge of the work

I’m Dario Hudon-Verrelli — a digital marketer, builder, writer, and solo operator based in Calgary, Alberta.

This blog is a place for public notes, project reflections, working ideas, and the occasional long-form thought I want to keep somewhere more permanent than a feed.

I write about marketing, storytelling, entrepreneurship, AI, community projects, maps, systems, creative work, and the strange process of learning in public.

Some of this will be polished. Some of it will be closer to a field note. The point is to keep a record of what I’m noticing, building, questioning, and trying to understand.

## What I’m interested in

* How stories shape what people believe
* How small teams can use better systems without becoming robotic
* How AI changes creative, strategic, and operational work
* How public data, maps, and simple tools can help people understand local issues
* How entrepreneurship feels when you are building with limited resources, curiosity, and lived experience

## Current projects

I run Octopus & Son Social Media Inc., a digital marketing agency focused on strategy, storytelling, design, advertising, photography, videography, web design, and copywriting.

I also build small public tools and experiments when an idea feels useful enough to make real.
